## How to Help

If this book helped you, consider paying it forward:

**Share it.** Send the link to someone who might need it. Post it where people in crisis might find it. Leave a copy at a shelter, library, or day center.

**Donate.** If you are in a position to give, your support helps keep this book free and funds future editions with updated resources.
