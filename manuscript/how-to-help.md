# How to Help

This book is free. It will always be free. Nobody should have to pay for survival information.

If this book helped you, or if you want to help someone else find it, here is how:

## Share It

Send the link to someone who needs it. Post it on social media. Print a copy and leave it at a shelter, a library, or a day center. The more people who have this information, the fewer people sleep outside unprepared.

## Support the Work

If you are in a position to contribute, donations help keep this book updated and free:

**Buy Me a Coffee:** [buymeacoffee.com/noquarter](https://buymeacoffee.com/noquarter)

Every dollar goes toward:
- Keeping the book free and updated
- Researching current prices, programs, and legal changes
- Creating country-specific editions (UK, Canada, Australia, EU)
- Printing physical copies for shelters and outreach workers

## Volunteer

If you work in homeless services, social work, or legal aid and want to contribute corrections, updates, or local resources — reach out. This book gets better with every pair of eyes on it.

## Report Errors

Prices change. Programs end. Phone numbers go dead. If you find something wrong in this book, report it so we can fix it for the next person.
